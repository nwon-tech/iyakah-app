Our Problem Statement

The principles of digital citizenship in Malaysia are increasingly challenged with the worry surge in misinformation. This website aims to empower Malaysian digital citizens, especially students, with the necessary skills to differentiate trustworthy information from deceptive or misleading content. This website will leverage machine learning algorithms, natural language processing and image recognition techniques for the functions offered. Educational resources will also be included in the website where users can be made aware of the various techniques on how to spot deceptive content and what to do about it. This comprehensive approach aims to not only enhance digital literacy but also build a resilient community.
